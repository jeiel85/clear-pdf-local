ClearPDF Local v1.6.1 — glare reduction
- If small light reflections got caught (e.g. on glossy paper), tap "Reduce glare" on a scanned page to soften them.
- It only handles small reflections; large blown-out white areas can't be recovered.
- Everything runs on-device with no internet.
